Kammavar USA

  • Kammavar USA Association Inc here in afterwards referred as KammavarUSA., is a registered 501(c)(3) community based Organization to provide services through social, cultural and charity work to all Tamil Nadu based Kammavar community in United States, Canada, India and worldwide.
  • The Organization shall be charitable, non-political, non-profit, cultural and social organization to preserve and protect Tamil Nadu based Kammavar community â€˜s heritage and culture for future generation , also to act upon concerning the welfare of community members.
  • Any individual aged 18 or above living in US , originated from Tamil Nadu Kammavar community and their kids (at -least one parent has to be kammavar) can become the members of the Association. Each individual has to register in the Association Portal to become a member of the Association.
  • The organization structure is purely based on volunteers under no circumstances the influences of any stream is encouraged.
We connect Tamilnadu based kammavar people in North America and Canada

Our Mission

To bring all kammas together in the most unexpected, surprising, and delightful ways possible. As it turns out, there are a lot more of us in United States and Canada than one would have imagined rethink this.

Our Plan

We will organize many events throughout the year to increase interactions between member families and establish a safe haven for every family to discuss any problems they require assistance with, which we will then provide.

Our Vision

A unified family in which all members of our kammavar organization shall participate to help one another out with ease and certainty.
